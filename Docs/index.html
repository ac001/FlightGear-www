FGFS Tutorials

Additional tutorials are always welcome. FlightGear has a zillion features that aren't immediately obvious. Tutorials can be a great way to point out some of the more subtle features and details of FlightGear.
  • A Circuit in FlightGear uses over 20 screenshots to show how to fly the FlightGear Cessna 172 smoothly by concentrating on attitudes.
  • Envoyez-vous en l'air ! - A FlightGear tutorial in French.

FGFS Papers and Presentations

Several papers have been published that center on or at least reference FlightGear. As we can provide copies or links, I will add them here:
  • Here are two papers presented at the European Space Agency ASTRA conference 2002 (ASTRA = Advanced Space Technologies in Robotics and Automation.)
    Landmark Recognition for Localization and Navigation of Aerial Vehicles
    Determination of Planetary Meteorology from Aerobot Flight Sensors

  • FlightGear was presented at the May 7, 2002 LUGOD meeting in Davis, CA. You can view the meeting minutes. You can also view a collections of photos from the presentation. Also available are the presentation slides.

  • AIAA 2002-0817 - Icing Encounter Flight Simulator - Written by Bipin Sehgal, Robert Deters, and Michael Selig.

  • The FlightGear Flight Simulator History, Status and Future - [html] [pdf] - slides from Alex Perry's talk at LinuxTag 2001

  • FlightGear visual scenery - [html] [pdf] - the slides from Alex Perry's talk at UKUUG Linux 2001 - Linux Developers' Conference

  • Using PCs in Flight Simulation Research - Presented at a flight simulation conference, May 24 - 25, 2001. Moscow, Russia.

  • Flight Gear - The Flight Simulator for free? - Written by Michael Basler, 1998.

  • Flight Gear - noch (k)ein neuer Flugsimulator - Written by Michael Basler, 1997. Comment: This gives a really interesting historical perspective on how far this project has come since the early days.
