FGFS Mirrors

Feel free to visit any of the FlightGear mirrors, but you'll likely have fastest load times if you pick one that's closest to you.

If you are interested in setting up additional mirror sights please contact . At last count, the web pages consume about 45Mb, and the basic ftp data consumes about 180Mb.

Web Page Mirrors

Location URL Comments
USA, Minnesota http://www.flightgear.org or http://www.fgfs.org master site
USA, Minnesota http://seneca.flightgear.org/flightgear/ good alternate
USA, North Carolina http://www.ibiblio.org/pub/mirrors/flightgear/www/ good alternate
USA, SourceForge http://flightgear.sf.net alternate
Germany http://www.de.flightgear.org/ www
