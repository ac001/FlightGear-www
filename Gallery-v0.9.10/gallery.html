On occasion FlightGear is spotting doing interesting things in the real world. Here FlightGear is being used as a real time synthetic display for a University of Minnesota UAV project. The two images (live video + synthetic view) were captured at the same time from the same perspective. You can see how closely they match. One of the goals of this project is to pilot the aircraft solely from the synthetic view. More details on this project can be found starting on my home page in my 'news' postings.
