FlightGear Aircraft Downloads

To install these aircraft, download the .tgz archive to your local hard drive, and extract them into your Aircraft folder. For windows users, this will be located in C:\Program Files\FlightGear\data\Aircraft

If you need a .tgz extractor for windows, consider http://www.7-zip.org/
