FGFS Older News

Here are a collection of older news flashes, most recent first.
  • December 20, 2002: Two papers were recently presented at the European Space Agency ASTRA 2002 conference. Both projects used FlightGear as the frame work for plugging in their research. One paper involves navigation of autonomous vehicles on foreign planets using terrain recognition; the other discusses the development of a fuzzy autopilot designed to control an autonomous flying vehicle operating in the Mars atmosphere. Both can be found in the Papers section of the Documenation page
  • December 10, 2002: The prebuilt Win32 binary for Version 0.9.1 is now (at long last) available. Download here.
  • December 10, 2002: Version 0.9.1 of the base package (textures, models, data, and config files) is finally available. Sorry for the delay. Download here.
